Story

Gojo Satoru comes from a prominent clan and is a descendant of the legendary sorcerer,
Michizane Sugawara (like Yuta in Jujutsu Kaisen).
As the only individual in 400 years to inherit both the “Six Eyes”
and “Limitless” skills, Satoru is regarded as the pride of the Gojo family in the Jujutsu world.
He is presently considered the world’s most powerful jujutsu sorcerer.
